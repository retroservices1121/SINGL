## Overview

SINGL is a single-event prediction market terminal that wraps Polymarket's deep liquidity ($9B+ annual volume) with a purpose-built trading experience. When a major event captures the world's attention — an election, a championship, an awards show, a macro crisis — SINGL becomes the war room where traders go deep on that one event with every data feed, visualization, and trading tool pointed at the same thing.

The platform is live and in production with real on-chain trading, automated data pipelines, portfolio management, and competitive social features.

---

## The Opportunity

Prediction markets are experiencing a breakout moment. Polymarket surpassed $9B in trading volume in 2024, driven by the US presidential election ($3.2B on a single event). Kalshi secured CFTC approval for election contracts. Bloomberg, CNN, and the New York Times now cite prediction market odds as authoritative.

Yet the user experience remains fragmented. Polymarket lists thousands of markets with no contextual data. Kalshi requires full KYC and bank linking. Neither platform is optimized for the intense, focused trading that happens when a single event dominates public attention.

SINGL fills this gap: a curated, event-driven terminal that combines Polymarket's liquidity with real-time news, social media, live scores, and competitive gamification — all focused on one event at a time.

---

## Product

**Adaptive Event Engine** — The entire platform reconfigures around the active event. Tournament brackets for March Madness. Candidate timelines for elections. Price tickers for economic events. Admin sets the event; the system auto-imports markets, tunes data feeds, and adapts the UI.

**Real-Time Data Pipeline** — Market prices from Polymarket CLOB (every 5 min), live scores via ESPN (every 30 sec), news aggregation (every 30 min), X/Twitter posts (every 15 min), YouTube and TikTok content (hourly). All automated, all contextual.

**Trading Infrastructure** — One-click Buy Yes/No with preset amounts ($10-$250). Gnosis Safe smart wallets provisioned automatically via Privy auth (email, social, or crypto wallet). USDC settlement on Polygon. Full position management: buy, sell before expiration, redeem after resolution.

**Social & Competitive Layer** — Global leaderboard with prize pools. Shareable Twitter/X cards for positions and portfolio performance. Upset alert system detecting underdog surges. Odds movement tracking with sparkline charts.

## Why Build on Polymarket?

Building on Polymarket is a deliberate strategic choice, not a constraint. Polymarket has invested hundreds of millions to build deep, liquid prediction markets across every major category. Recreating that liquidity from scratch would require massive capital and years of market-making.

SINGL focuses where value accrues differently: **curation, UX, distribution, and social engagement.** This mirrors successful models across fintech — Robinhood on market makers, DeFi aggregators on Uniswap, neobanks on core banking platforms. The interface layer captures the user relationship and the margin.

Polymarket actively benefits from frontends driving volume. As the ecosystem matures, multiple funded projects are building specialized interfaces on Polymarket liquidity, validating this approach.

## Competitive Position

SINGL does not compete on liquidity (Polymarket), regulation (Kalshi), or breadth (DraftKings). SINGL competes on **focus, experience, and virality** — the terminal you open when the event everyone's watching is live and you want to trade it with conviction.

| | Polymarket | Kalshi | SINGL |
