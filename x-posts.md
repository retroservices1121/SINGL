## Post 2 — The Concept

Most prediction markets show you everything. SINGL shows you one thing.

One active event. All related markets aggregated. News, social, video — all tuned to that event. Price charts, odds movement, alerts — all contextual.

When the event ends, the next one loads.

Hyperfocused trading.

## Post 4 — How It Works

Connect wallet. See the event. Trade the outcomes.

SINGL pulls every related market from Polymarket into one view. You see odds, charts, news, and social — all for the same event. Buy Yes or No. Sell before expiration. Redeem winnings.

Trades execute on Polygon in USDC via Gnosis Safe. No custody.
