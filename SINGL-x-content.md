**Thread 3 — "How SINGL Works Under the Hood" (5 tweets)**

1/ People ask how SINGL works technically. Here's the stack behind a single prediction trade on SINGL. 🧵

2/ You sign in with email (or wallet). Privy handles auth and automatically provisions a Gnosis Safe smart wallet on Polygon.

No seed phrases. No MetaMask popups. Your wallet is created silently.

3/ When you trade, SINGL routes your order through the Synthesis Trading SDK to Polymarket's CLOB (Central Limit Order Book).

Your USDC goes into the smart wallet. The trade executes on-chain. Position recorded in our database.

4/ Meanwhile, 5 data feeds run on cron:

→ Polymarket prices (5 min)
→ News articles (30 min)
→ X/Twitter posts (15 min)
→ YouTube (1 hr)
→ Live ESPN scores (30 sec)

Everything contextual to the active event.

5/ When you sell or the market resolves, USDC settles back to your wallet.

Your P&L is tracked. Your position is shareable. Your rank updates on the leaderboard.

Full cycle. On-chain. One click.
